Welcome To Hyderabad Blood Donors

Every Year Thousands of units of blood is required in and around Hyderabad & Secunderabad. HyderabadBloodDonors.org is an online platform where donors and recipients or patients could meet together. Our charitable blood donors donate blood for emergency need, the needy and natural calamities.

HyderabadBloodDonors.org which is under the aegis of Lions Club Of Secunderabad Pearls is a non-profit, non-commercial online boundary of social dedication which is a device to use the power of the Internet to help common people and does not deal in blood nor is it a blood bank.
